Ducks are another common choice for homesteaders. On average, ducks are larger than chickens; therefore they also lay larger eggs. Making them a better investment for small operations that only want to maintain a couple animals. With their increased size, comes an increased appetite, and they typically require an area where they can swim. Ducks are better suited to free range conditions, and rarely do well in small spaces.
